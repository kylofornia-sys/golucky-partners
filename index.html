Supply Agreement Terms

Transparency first. Here are the terms that govern every Go Lucky partnership.

Agreement Conditions

Standing Order: Tier 1 partners agree to a minimum weekly standing order. Adjustments must be communicated 48 hours prior to delivery.
Retention of Title: Ownership of all goods remains with Go Lucky Free Range until full payment has been received.
Cession of Proceeds: If goods are sold prior to payment, the proceeds are deemed held in trust for Go Lucky Free Range and are ceded as security for the debt.
Automatic Suspension: Supply is automatically suspended if payment is not received by the due date (e.g., bounced debit order or late EFT). Reinstatement may require COD terms.
Price Adjustments: Go Lucky reserves the right to adjust the Base Price in line with farm gate cost increases. Partners will be notified 7 days in advance.
Late Payment Interest: Tier 2 & 3 accounts are subject to 2.5% per month interest on any late payments beyond agreed terms.
Freshness Guarantee: Fresh or we replace — every delivery, every time. If you're not happy with the quality, we make it right.
