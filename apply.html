Supply Agreement Terms

Agreement Conditions

Pricing & Terms: Egg pricing and payment terms are agreed directly between you and Go Lucky for your account. The preference you select below tells us how you'd like to settle.
Retention of Title: Ownership of all goods remains with Go Lucky Free Range until full payment has been received.
Cession of Proceeds: If goods are sold prior to payment, the proceeds are deemed held in trust for Go Lucky Free Range and are ceded as security for the debt.
Automatic Suspension: Supply may be suspended if payment is not received by the agreed due date. Reinstatement may require settlement on delivery.
Price Adjustments: Go Lucky reserves the right to adjust pricing in line with farm gate cost increases. Clients will be notified 7 days in advance.
Freshness Guarantee: Fresh or we replace — every delivery, every time. If you're not happy with the quality, we make it right.
